Example Description

The FEKO-A suite is developed at UCLA VLSI CAD LAB [1,2].  8 floorplanning examples were generated starting from originals from the GSRC and MCNC suite. Their only difference is the aspect ratios of the blocks, which are set to values that guarantee a zero deadspace solution for them. Using these examples, one can evaluate the performance of floorplanning algorithms in terms of area minimization. Table 1 gives the characteristics of the FEKO-A suite and the performance of four floorplanning algorithms [3,4,5,6] on the FEKO-A examples. The examples can be downloaded here.

Table 1. The  FEKO-A suite. Column "Orig" shows the initial circuit from which the synthetic circuits are derived.  Column Blocks shows the number of blocks on the circuit. The remaining columns show the performance of floorplanning algorithms (Parquet-2 ,  TCG-S, B*-tree and BloBB) in deadspace and runtime on the FEKO-A examples. The running times are on a Pentium 4 2.4 GHz processor except for TCG-S (750 MHz on Solaris).
